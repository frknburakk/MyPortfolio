About Me

Ben Furkan Burak Temel, I'm a third-year Digital Game Design student at Muğla Sıtkı Koçman University. I enjoy creating games and visuals. For the past two years, I’ve been making 3D models using Blender and joined school projects as a 3D artist. I’m also learning Unity and can make simple games with basic 2D animations. Recently, I started learning Adobe Substance to improve my texturing skills. I have experience designing UI for school projects, and I'm always working to improve. I’m open to learning, growing, and creating. Welcome to my portfolio!
